While my passion lies in the intricacies of back-end engineering, I recognize the value of a full-stack perspective and actively seek opportunities to enhance my front-end capabilities.

However, after Codeup’s unexpected permanent shutdown, I was presented with the challenge to continue and actively develop my abilities through independent projects, as demonstrated on this page.

This personal webpage is a work in progress and I will continue to add more projects and make it look better. Please have a look through.
